Is your Facebook Password Secure?

Odds are, either you or a friend have wound up on the wrong end of a hacked Facebook account. It turns out that this happens far more than you'd probably expect: New official statistics from the social network have revealed that more than 600,000 are hacked on a daily basis
In other words, every 140 milliseconds, a Facebook account is hacked. (source)

You can easily protect your account by following few very simple rules. Entering your Facebook credentials will run advanced analytics algorithm to determine if your password unique and strong enough.

Hint: it isn't.
